## Primary Caption

Your day should not begin by accident and end by exhaustion.

In the new Industrious Framework chapter, **Waking and Sleeping with Discipline**, we focus on two daily anchors: a consistent wake time and a deliberate bedtime.

When those boundaries are stable, discipline becomes easier. The day has a beginning. The night has a close. The schedule between them has a structure to return to.

Wake with intention. Sleep with intention. Build the day between them.
